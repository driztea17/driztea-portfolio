Goals

1 - High Quality Designs

Understanding, perceiving, and using a product is made obvious with an appealing design.Good designs explain what a product does. Furthermore, by utilizing the user's intuition, the product is able to express its purpose clearly. My goal is to create user-friendly designs that are popular with a large number of people.

2 - Web Development

The process of developing a website for the Internet or an intranet is known as web development. You can develop web applications, electronic businesses, or social network services from simple static text pages to complex web applications. In order to make my UI/UX designs realistic and functional, I am studying web development.

3 - Animated Illustrations

The term illustration describes still visuals such as drawings in a book or paintings on a poster. I create illustrations and then plan to create animations based on those illustrations. Animation can be drawn or generated by a computer, and as I create illustrations, my next step is to convert those illustrations into animations.

4 - Tiny Room Designs

Blender is used to create tiny rooms, 3D structures of random rooms or architectures. Architects use it for planning or to illustrate a 3D mock-up of their project. The idea of creating 3D illustrations using blender, such as tiny rooms, is very intriguing to me so I want to learn it.
